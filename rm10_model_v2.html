H2 budget-constrained model

H2 budget is capped at RM470k. This model starts from the budget: it caps enrolment so total payouts fit, then shows the reach, impact, and duration you can buy. Adjust the cap, run length, and assumptions to find the pilot shape you want.

Sizing model, not a forecast. Benchmarks are published industry priors (AppsFlyer, Adjust, Statista, loyalty/gamification literature), not verified against live Mudah data. Baseline: app new users 203,273/mo (Android + iOS, Jun 26). At full new-user scale the reward would cost ~RM288k/month — so the budget is the binding constraint and enrolment must be capped.
